MAPPING RETRACTED NETWORKS

Take a look at some of the networks that span across the globe. Here you can find different authors and zoom into their connections.May 25th, 2024

There are many reasons why a paper could be retracted. This project, however, is particularly looking at those authors, who were retracted and received a publishingban from Journals. This map gives an overview of connections between China and Iran in a papermill scandal and many more.
